CapyPic

Fetch capybara pictures in your Discord server!

Features

Get Capybara Pictures

With CapyPic's easy /capybara command, you can get a new picture of a capybara with ease. We use the capy.lol API for capybara pictures.

Capybara Facts

CapyPic can provide you with a multitude of fun facts about capybaras! Suggest new facts in our support server!

Reliable Uptime

You can count on CapyPic to be up and running almost always. CapyPic is currently hosted on my personal server.
